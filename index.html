Guansheng Li

My research aims to develop mathematically grounded and computationally efficient frameworks for multiscale biological systems by integrating applied mathematics, scientific machine learning, and data-driven modeling. I focus on blood flow, red blood cell biomechanics, and disease-related dynamics such as sickle cell disease, combining physics-based modeling, stochastic processes, and AI methods to enable predictive and interpretable biomedical simulations.
