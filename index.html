Welcome

Welcome to the MBCAI Lab. My research lies at the interface of applied mathematics, scientific machine learning, computational science, and biomedicine. I focus on multiscale modeling, blood flow and red blood cell biomechanics, and AI-driven biomedical simulations.

Research

Scientific Machine Learning

Blood Flow and RBC Biomechanics

Computational modeling of blood flow, red blood cell mechanics, and sickle cell disease.

Biomedical AI

AI-based image analysis for microfluidic assays and biomedical applications.
