Open Positions

The BioComp Group is seeking motivated PhD students in areas including multiscale biological modeling, scientific machine learning, blood flow, red blood cell biomechanics, and biomedical computation.

Applicants should have strong computational and programming skills, with backgrounds in applied mathematics, computational science, fluid mechanics, physics, computer science, biomedical engineering, or related disciplines. Experience in numerical methods, scientific machine learning, or high-performance computing is a plus.

To apply or inquire, please contact Prof. Guansheng Li at guansheng_li@brown.edu with your CV, transcripts, and a brief description of your research interests.
