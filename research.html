Research

Our group develops mathematically grounded, computationally driven, and data-informed approaches for biological and biomedical systems. Click on each research direction below to learn more.

Multiscale Biological Modeling and Simulation

Mathematical and computational frameworks for multiscale biological systems, spanning cellular mechanics, transport phenomena, and complex physiological processes.

Scientific Machine Learning

Physics-informed learning, data-driven modeling, and interpretable AI methods for biological systems, biomedical data, and predictive scientific computing.

Interdisciplinary Scientific Computing in Health and Disease

Computational approaches for blood flow, red blood cell biomechanics, disease dynamics, and translational problems in biomedical science and engineering.
